Our Specialties

Discover our wide variety of authentic Nigerian dishes

Swallows

Iyan (Pounded Yam)

Soft, smooth pounded yam served with your choice of soup.

Amala

Traditional yam flour swallow with rich, flavorful soups.

Semo

Fine semolina swallow that pairs perfectly with any soup.

Fufu

Cassava-based swallow with a unique texture and flavor.

Ebiripo

Local specialty swallow made from fermented cassava.

Eba

Garri-based swallow that's a Nigerian favorite.

Soups & Stews

Efo Riro

Rich vegetable soup with assorted meat and fish.

Egusi

Melon seed soup with assorted meat and fish.

Ewedu

Smooth jute leaves soup, perfect with Amala.
